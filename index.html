The Podium Club

Helping individuals improve public speaking skills.

We are a public speaking club based in Arusha, Tanzania with a mission to improve the public speaking skills of our members. Through workshops, regular practice opportunities, and feedback from experienced speakers, we help our members become more confident and comfortable speaking in front of others.

Programs

The Podium Club offers a variety of programs to help our members improve their public speaking skills.

  • Workshops
  • Podcasts
  • Handouts
  • Networking Events
  • Lightning Talks
  • Debates
